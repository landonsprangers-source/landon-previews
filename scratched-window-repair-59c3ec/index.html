The process

Four steps. No replacement.

Every project follows the same precision workflow. Most jobs wrap on-site in a single day.

Step 01

Assess

We inspect the damage depth, glass type, and surrounding finish — on-site or by photo. You get a clear, written estimate. If replacement is genuinely the better call, we'll tell you.

Step 02

Mask & protect

Surrounding surfaces, fixtures, and finishes are masked and sealed. We work clean — your space looks the same when we leave.

Step 03

Polish & restore

Multi-stage cerium and diamond slurries. Each pass removes depth in microns — no distortion, no haze, no telltale halo. Just the surface, restored.

Step 04

Inspect & guarantee

We walk the surface with you. Light test, mirror test, hand on glass. Not happy with the result? We come back. You don't pay for re-work.

Frequently asked

Real questions, straight answers.

Can you really fix it without replacing the glass?
Yes — for the vast majority of surface damage. Our process removes depth in microns until the scratch, etch, or stain is gone. The original glass stays in the frame.
How long does a typical project take?
Most residential and auto projects wrap in a few hours. Storefronts and commercial work usually finish in a day. Multi-pane jobs take longer, but you keep operating — no demolition, no install crew.
Will there be distortion or haze after?
No. We polish to optical flatness and verify with light tests before we leave. If you can see where the damage was, we haven't finished.
What surfaces beyond glass do you restore?
Stainless steel (appliances, panels, Tesla bodies), polished stone, mirrors, aquarium glass, shower doors, and select specialty surfaces. If you're unsure, send us a photo.
Do you work with insurance for vandalism or damage claims?
Often, yes. We document the damage with photos and detailed scope, and bill direct when carriers approve restoration in lieu of replacement. It usually saves them money too.
Will you travel for commercial projects outside SoCal?
Yes. We've worked nationally on storefronts, hotel chains, and large commercial properties. If the project's big enough that restoration beats replacement, the travel makes sense.
How much will it cost?
A fraction of replacement. Most clients land 30–70% below their replacement quote. Send us photos and your quote — we estimate fast and 99% of the time we beat it.
